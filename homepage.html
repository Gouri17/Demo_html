ABOUT US

Founder

The LTI has been founded by Mr. Rafik Salim Shaikh with the help of Mr. Sudhir Palsule Sir the CEO of Leena Engineering Works. The Institute started with the broad vision to educate students in automation and conventional machineries, and also help them to develop skills and specialization in various aspects of industry.




Established

Our institute was established in 2012, we have made progress in terms of expert quality teaching and improving skills in candidates. Since year 2012 near about 400-500 students successfully completed CNC Automation program under our guidance. We also helped candidates to get placed in various industries as CNC operator,Programmer,Setter,Engineer, Supervisor etc.
